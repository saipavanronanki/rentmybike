Why ?

We simplified RentMyBike, so you can focus on what's important to you.

Safe Rides

Your safety is our priority. From sanitizing all bikes before every use, to extensive on-ground safety measures, your rides with ONN will always be safe and reliable. We also offer helmets on-demand.

Flexible Ownership

Enjoy the freedom of owning a bike without the hefty down-payments, EMIs and paperwork. Now choose from rent-to-own, monthly/quarterly bookings, and even daily plans.

Smarter Mobility

With your trusty ONN app, you can choose any bike, make instant payments, get offers, and manage all aspects of your ONN experience right from the comfort and ease of your mobile app.

ONN Stations

Your local ONN Station is here to make ensure your RentMyBike experience is flawless. At your neighborhood station, you get to pick any bike, get maintenance, sanitized, and a lot more.
